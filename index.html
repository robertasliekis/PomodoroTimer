WHAT IS POMODORO TIMER?

Online Pomodoro Timer

It is a customizable and easy to use looping timer to boost your working efficiency.

About Pomodoro Technique

Pomodoroâ„¢ Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. This technique use timer to break down work into a set of intervals separated by breaks. Pomodoro technique increases productivity by taking short scheduled breaks regularly.

How to use Pomodoro timers

  • Set timer to 25 minutes for one Pomodoro
  • Work on the task until timer is complete
  • After timer completion take a 5 minutes short break
  • After four Pomodoro's take a long break
  • Repeat from step 1

Use the loop button to do step 1 until step 4 in a row
